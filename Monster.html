What Is inside a Mosnter Energy Drink

This is What is Inside a Monster Energy Drink!

Ingredients

  • Caffeine (160mg per 16 fl oz can)
  • Sugar (54g per 16 fl oz can)
  • Taurine
  • B Vitamins (B2, B3, B6, B12)
  • L-Carnitine
  • Ginseng Root Extract
  • Glucuronolactone
  • Carbonated Water
  • Artificial Flavors & Preservatives
  • Citric Acid

Instructions

  1. Mix 1 cup of carbonated water with 1/2 cup of sugar.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of citric acid and stir well.
  3. Mix in 1/4 teaspoon of caffeine and a drop of food coloring.
  4. Put in fridge for 1 hour to let freeze enjoy!
